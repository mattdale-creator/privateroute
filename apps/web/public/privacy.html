Privacy

P0 policy — matches actual logging.

  • We store account email, password hash, device public keys, assigned VPN IPs, and provision events.
  • P0 may store device private keys to re-download configs (convenience). Prefer rotating devices if concerned.
  • We do not store full packet contents of VPN traffic.
  • Payment: not wired in P0 (auto-activate). Stripe will add customer/payment IDs in P1.
